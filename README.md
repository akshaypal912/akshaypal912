## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📊 [Music Stream Data Engineering Project](https://github.com/akshaypal912/Music-Stream-DataEngineer-project)
A production-style Data Engineering workflow — from raw CSV ingestion through Python-based data cleaning, cloud database loading on AWS EC2, and advanced SQL analytics on a music streaming dataset.

- **Goal:** Build a complete data pipeline: ingest raw play data, clean it with Python & Pandas, load into cloud-hosted PostgreSQL, and extract insights using SQL joins, aggregations, and window functions.
- **Tools:** `Python` `PostgreSQL` `AWS EC2` `Pandas` `SQL`
- **Highlights:** 14 raw records ingested → 10 clean records loaded to DB | 7 SQL queries written | 3 tables & 3 indexes created

---

### 📈 [End-to-End E-commerce Revenue Intelligence & Analytics Pipeline](https://github.com/akshaypal912/End-to-End-E-commerce-Revenue-Intelligence-and-Analytics-Pipeline)
A real-world business intelligence workflow — from raw data ingestion to executive-level dashboard reporting, covering data engineering, SQL analytics, KPIs, customer segmentation, and revenue forecasting.

- **Dataset:** 5,000 synthetic transactions | 30 products | 2,859 unique customers | May 2025 – May 2026
- **Tools:** `Python` `PostgreSQL` `Power BI` `SQL` `ETL`
- **Highlights:** Identified top-selling categories, low-performing months, and revenue concentration risks to drive strategic business decisions

---

### 🔍 [Trader Sentiment Analysis](https://github.com/akshaypal912/trader_sentiment_analysis)
Analyzed whether market sentiment — Fear, Neutral, or Greed — has a measurable impact on trader PnL, win rate, and trade size to extract actionable strategy insights from real trading data.

- **Tools:** `Python` `Pandas` `Jupyter` `Power BI`
- **Highlights:** Greed → higher PnL & win rate | Fear → higher losses & lower win rate | Large trades generate significantly higher returns
